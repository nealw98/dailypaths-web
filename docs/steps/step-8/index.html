The Twelve Steps / Step 8
Step 8

Made a list of all persons we had harmed, and became willing to make amends to them all.

August · Brotherly Love

Questions for Reflection

Take your time with these questions. There are no right answers — only honest ones. You may want to write your thoughts in a journal or share them with your sponsor or a trusted friend.

  • Who have I harmed through my reactions to someone else’s drinking?
  • Am I on my own list of people I’ve harmed?
  • What is the difference between willingness and action in this step?
  • Are there people I resist putting on my list? What does that resistance tell me?
  • How do I distinguish between harm I’ve caused and harm done to me?
