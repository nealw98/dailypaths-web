The Twelve Steps

A Framework for Personal Freedom

1 Honesty

Admitting powerlessness to reclaim your life.

 2 Hope

Finding a path toward a restored perspective.

 3 Faith

The decision to release the burden of control.

 4 Courage

A fearless look into the mirror of the soul.

 5 Integrity

The healing power of radical honesty.

 6 Willingness

Preparing the heart for lasting change.

 7 Humility

The humble request for a new way of being.

 8 Brotherly Love

Mapping the journey toward reconciliation.

 9 Justice

The courage to make things right.

 10 Perseverance

The daily practice of self-awareness.

 11 Spiritual Awareness

Seeking conscious contact through stillness.

 12 Service

Carrying the message; living the principles.

Carry the Path with You.

Get the full library of Step readings and personal journaling tools in the Al-Anon Daily Paths App.

The Twelve Steps are adapted from Alcoholics Anonymous and used by Al-Anon Family Groups. For the official Al-Anon perspective on the Steps, visit al-anon.org.
