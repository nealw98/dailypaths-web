The Twelve Steps

A path to personal recovery for anyone affected by someone else’s drinking — guiding us from powerlessness toward a spiritual awakening.

Each step builds on the one before it. Working these steps with a sponsor or someone who has walked the path before you can make all the difference — their experience and encouragement help bring the steps to life.

Al-Anon’s Paths to Recovery is the definitive guide to working the Steps, Traditions, and Concepts of Service. Each month’s reflections on this site focus on one step, from Step 1 in January through Step 12 in December.

1 Honesty

We admitted we were powerless over alcohol — that our lives had become unmanageable.
