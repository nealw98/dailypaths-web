Step 4

Made a searching and fearless moral inventory of ourselves.

April · Courage

Questions for Reflection

Take your time with these questions. There are no right answers — only honest ones. You may want to write your thoughts in a journal or share them with your sponsor or a trusted friend.

  • What character traits have helped me survive difficult situations?
  • What patterns in my behavior have caused harm to myself or others?
  • Am I willing to look honestly at both my strengths and my shortcomings?
  • What fears come up when I think about examining my own behavior?
  • How have the effects of someone else’s alcoholism shaped who I am today?
